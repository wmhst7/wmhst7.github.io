About

I am a Research Scientist at ByteDance Seed, working on systems for large-scale language models. My work focuses on scaling LLM pretraining through PyTorch-native graph compilation, distributed training optimization, performance modeling, and heterogeneous inference systems. During my master's studies, I was advised by Yunhao Liu. I received my M.S. from the University of Washington and my B.S. from Tsinghua University.
